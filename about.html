About Us

Technology has suffered a substantial amount of criticism in recent years due to major scandals in the tech industry. Nevertheless, we have to realize that despite its flaws, technology has formed humanity's collective identity. It has provided the world with the unimaginable. Without it, you wouldn't be here reading this today.

That is why I decided to create the Museum of Technology. An ode to humanity's greatest development.
